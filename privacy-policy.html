Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2025

At RememberBill, we prioritize your privacy above all else. Our approach is simple: your data stays on your device. This Privacy Policy explains how the RememberBill Chrome Extension ("RememberBill," "Extension," "we," "our," or "us") handles your information.

1. Local-First Philosophy

RememberBill is built on a local-first approach to privacy:

  • All your financial information and bill data are stored exclusively on your device using your browser's secure local storage.
  • No sign-up, account creation, or personal information collection is required to use RememberBill.
  • Your data never leaves your device unless you specifically choose to export it.

2. Information We Don't Collect

To be absolutely clear, RememberBill does not collect:

  • Your name, email, or any personal identifiers
  • Financial account information, balances, or transaction details
  • Bill amounts, due dates, or payment history
  • Browsing history or website activity outside the Extension
  • Location data, IP addresses, or device information

3. Optional Analytics

To help improve the Extension, we offer an optional analytics program:

  • Analytics are opt-in and can be enabled or disabled via the Settings panel at any time.
  • If enabled, we collect anonymous usage data through Google Analytics, including:
    • Feature usage patterns (which features you use most)
    • Bill counts and basic interaction metrics (without bill details)
    • Extension open frequency and feature engagement
    • General workflow patterns to identify areas for improvement
  • We generate a random anonymous ID for analytics purposes that cannot be linked to you personally.
  • We never collect bill names, amounts, due dates, or any personal financial details.
  • Analytics data is transmitted securely using HTTPS to our analytics provider.
  • This data helps us understand how people use RememberBill so we can make it better for everyone.

You can view your current analytics preference and change it anytime by:

  1. Opening RememberBill
  2. Clicking the Settings icon (⚙️) in the top right
  3. Going to the "Analytics & Privacy" section
  4. Toggling "Allow Anonymous Usage Data" on or off

4. How Your Data Is Stored

RememberBill uses your browser's built-in local storage mechanisms:

  • Data is stored using IndexedDB and localStorage, standard browser storage technologies.
  • Your information remains within your browser environment.
  • We recommend creating periodic manual backups of your data through the Export feature.

5. Browser Permissions

RememberBill requires the following browser permissions:

  • Storage: To save your bill information locally on your device.
  • Notifications: To send you bill reminders and alerts.
  • Alarms: To schedule reminders and check for upcoming bills.

We request only the minimum permissions necessary for functionality.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Since all data is stored locally:

  • Your data remains on your device until you choose to delete it.
  • Uninstalling the Extension will permanently delete all associated data.
  • You can clear all data through the Extension settings at any time.

7. Security

While we've designed RememberBill with security in mind:

  • Your data's security depends partly on your browser's security and your device settings.
  • We recommend keeping your browser updated and using device security features like screen locks.
  • We don't have access to your data and cannot recover it if lost.

8. Children's Privacy

RememberBill is not intended for use by individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. When we make changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
